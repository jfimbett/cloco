Research question: Does W1(μ_i, ν) explain cross-sectional leverage heterogeneity beyond mean and variance?

**Why:** The paper introduces capital supply distribution ν as a new structural primitive; prior models use scalar r_f. Key theoretical result: optimal debt contract = quantile coupling, debt capacity = W1(μ_i, ν)/2.
