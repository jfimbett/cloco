## Project
"Liquid Assets" — Imbet, Kräussl, Piatti, Steri (Paris Dauphine, Bayes, QMUL, Luxembourg)
Research question: Why is the price-age profile of fine wine non-monotone? Structural model with two buyer types.
Data: 1,068,703 French fine-wine auction transactions 1996–2015, Bordeaux Crus Classés and Burgundy Premier/Grand Cru.

## Key Anchor Papers (Proximity 4–5)
- Dimson, Rousseau & Spaenjers (2015, JFE 118:431-449) — "The Price of Wine" — 4.1% real return; maturity kink; non-pecuniary collector benefits
- Breeden & Liang (2017, JWE 12:173-202) — APC model documents non-monotone cubic age-price profile
- Masset (2024, Economic Modelling 141:106915) — 5 market segments; lifecycle pricing
- Masset et al. (2016, Emerging Markets Review 29:200-225) — China/HK premium; 19% avg; collapses post-2012
- Cardebat et al. (2017, JWE 12:302-331) — Price dispersion; heterogeneous buyers
- Lovo & Spaenjers (2018, AER 108:744-774) — two-type art model; closest theoretical antecedent
- Mandel (2009, AER 99:1653-1663) — art as investment and conspicuous consumption good
